How to Use Your Dhaka Metro OnePass Ticket

Follow these simple steps to make your journey smooth and hassle-free:

Step 1: Scan and Enter

Scan your QR code at the entry gate to start your journey. The gate will open once your ticket is validated.

Step 2: Travel

Sit back, relax, and travel to your destination. Your ticket remains valid for the duration of your trip.

Step 3: Scan and Exit

At your destination, scan your ticket at the exit gate to complete your journey. The gate will open and you can exit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can fill out the form, and our team will get back to you within 24 hours. You can also reach us via email or phone listed at the bottom of this page.

Q: What is your response time?

A: Our usual response time is within 1-2 business days, depending on the nature of your inquiry.

Q: Can I visit your office in person?

A: Yes, you are welcome to visit our office during business hours. Please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment.

Q: Do you offer support during weekends?

A: We provide limited support during weekends. For urgent matters, please mark your inquiry as "urgent" in the contact form.
